EXIFrame

Privacy-Focused EXIF Editor for iOS

Take control of your photo metadata with EXIFrame. Edit, view, and remove EXIF data from your photos with complete privacy and ease.

About EXIFrame

EXIFrame is designed with privacy as its core principle. Unlike other EXIF editors, all photo processing happens locally on your device. Your photos never leave your iPhone, ensuring complete privacy and data control.
