Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

EXIFrame ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

No Personal Data Collection: EXIFrame does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All photo processing happens locally on your device.

Photos: The app only accesses photos that you explicitly select for editing. These photos are processed entirely on your device and are never uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties.

How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no data to use, share, or sell. Your photos remain completely private and under your control.

Data Storage

All data processing occurs locally on your iOS device. We do not have access to your photos, EXIF data, or any other information stored on your device.

Third-Party Services

EXIFrame does not integrate with any third-party services that would collect or transmit your personal data. The app operates entirely offline.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any information from children under 13. Since no data is collected at all, this policy applies to users of all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@exiframe.com

Summary

In simple terms: EXIFrame is designed with privacy as its core principle. We don't collect, store, or share any of your data. Your photos and their metadata remain completely private and under your control at all times.
