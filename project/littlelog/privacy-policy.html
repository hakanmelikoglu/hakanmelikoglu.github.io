Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

Baby Tracker ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and your child's privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

No Personal Data Collection: Baby Tracker does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data is stored locally on your device.

Baby Data: All information about your baby (feeding times, sleep patterns, growth measurements, etc.) is stored exclusively on your device using Core Data. This data is never uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties.

Photos: If you choose to add photos to your baby's profile or milestones, these are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded or shared.

How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no data to use, share, or sell. Your baby's data remains completely private and under your control at all times.

Data Storage

All data processing and storage occurs locally on your iOS device using Core Data. We do not have access to your baby's information, photos, or any other data stored in the app.

Data Export

The app provides functionality to export your data as PDF reports. This export process is performed entirely on your device, and the exported files are saved to your device's local storage or shared through iOS's standard sharing mechanisms.

Third-Party Services

Baby Tracker does not integrate with any third-party services that would collect or transmit your personal data. The app operates entirely offline.

Children's Privacy

Our app is designed to help parents track their children's development. Since no data is collected or transmitted, this policy applies to users of all ages. Parents are responsible for the data they choose to store in the app.

Data Backup

If you have iCloud backup enabled on your device, your app data may be included in your device's iCloud backup. This is controlled by your device settings, not by our app.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: exiframe@hakanmelikoglu.com

Summary

In simple terms: Baby Tracker is designed with privacy as its core principle. We don't collect, store, or share any of your data. Your baby's information remains completely private and under your control at all times. All data is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.
